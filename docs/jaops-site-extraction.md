## Extracted Product Content

### Yamcs Gateway

- Yamcs is presented as a powerful open-source mission control system used by companies and space agencies.
- JAOPS has experience with Yamcs since its inception and supports deployment/customization for development, test, simulation, and operations.
- Yamcs Gateway extends Yamcs into early development so telemetry-generating and command-receiving elements can connect early.
- Current interfaces: ROS2, CANopen, CAN-TS.
- Future interfaces mentioned: RS422, SpaceWire, Milbus.
- Benefits: unified procedures, displays, simulation blocks, automatic procedures, remote support, anomaly alarms, central mission data, replay/archive/monitor/control, lower recurring costs.

### Tools

- MCS for telemetry monitoring and control.
- Planning tool for context across past, present, and future activities.
- Knowledge management and document/change control.
- Data processing and analysis.
- Anomaly traceability with contextual data, fault tree analysis, and action traceability.
- Shift planning.
- VoIP for ground-station and customer communications.

### Simulation Environments

- Spacecraft orbit and lunar rover simulation environments.
- Supports design, validation, verification, training, and rehearsals.
- Provides realistic sensor data, telemetry/telecommand interfaces, integrated software testing, immersive operator training, photorealistic lunar rendering, physics, and rover functional models.

## Extracted Service Content

### Turnkey MCC

- JAOPS develops mission control centers worldwide.
- Focus areas: layout, infrastructure, processes, certification.
- Promise: ready-to-use, flight-proven facility in a short time.

### Operations as a Service

- Lets customers focus on products and customers while JAOPS handles operations.
- Reduces recruiting, ground software development/testing, and risk through experienced operators and flight-heritage software.
- Potential cost reductions shown on source page: 91% infrastructure, 83% tools, 44% personnel.
- Short-lead-time path: 6 months MCC IT, with staged MCC infrastructure, testing/training, simulation, and operations.
- Use cases: lunar rover operations, spacecraft operations, astronaut support, UAV operations.

### Operational Review

- JAOPS can participate in PDR, CDR, TRR, and ORR reviews as external reviewers.
- Early phase consulting embeds operational requirements and system engineering practices from the start.

### Training & Certification

- OPS certification emphasizes real-life applications, expert instructors, and confidence-building.
- Certification tracks: Flight, SPACOM, Ground Controller, Systems, Customer.
- Training blocks include mission overview, MCC infrastructure, OPS tools, subsystem-specific knowledge, and simulation.
- Also includes community engagement, STEM promotion, knowledge sharing, coaching, team building, and performance.
